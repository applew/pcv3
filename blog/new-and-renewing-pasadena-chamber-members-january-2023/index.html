New and Renewing Pasadena Chamber Members in January, 2023

The Pasadena Chamber has enjoyed the membership of many and diverse companies. We celebrate each new member and value those who renew their investment in the Chamber. 

At the Pasadena Chamber we value the loyalty of our members very much and are pleased so many continue as members, some for decades and some even for a century or more.

Thank you to Bryan Muth of Pasadena Private Wealth, Pat Rosengren of Huntington Marketing and Association Management, Preeya Poopichayapongse at Boba Cha Cha in Old Pasadena and Ruth Martinez in the City of Pasadena Economic Development Department for referring new members to the Chamber in October and November.

The person or company who refers the most new members in the Fiscal Year receives a full-color, 1/4 page ad in the printed Pasadena Chamber Business Director and the Pasadena Chamber Visitors Guide (a $1,285 value). The ad will appear in the front section (editorial section) of the publication.

These companies celebrated milestone anniversaries as Pasadena Chamber members:

  • FIVE YEAR ANNIVERSARIES: Air-Tech Air Conditioning & Heating, Inc.; Barry’s Ticket Service; California State University, Los Angeles
  • TEN YEAR ANNIVERSARIES: Citrust Insurance Agency; Domino’s Pizza-Colorado Blvd.; Twohey’s; Urth Caffe
  • THIRTY-FIVE YEAR ANNIVERSARY: J. Robin & Associates Graphic Design
