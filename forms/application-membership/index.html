Application for Membership

Invest the future of your business... join the Pasadena Chamber of Commerce!

We are California's most energetic business service and support organization!

Joining the Pasadena Chamber of Commerce may be the most cost-effective investment you can make in the prosperity of your business. While each company has a designated representative to the Pasadena Chamber, membership benefits are extended to every employee of a member company.

If you have questions about membership in the Pasadena Chamber of Commerce and Civic Association, please call 626-795-3355 or mail kelly@pasadena-chamber.org.

Click here to view the Pasadena_Chamber_Membership_Brochure_2022.pdf (PDF will open in a new window) then fill out the form below.

Join online or, if you prefer, you can fill out the PDF form and mail it in with your payment.

Minority owned or operated small businesses (25 or fewer employees) can join for $100 per year. For new memberships only.

Home Based Businesses can join for $230 per year.

If you are a member of another local chamber, and are not already a member of the Pasadena Chamber of Commerce, you can join at a one-year introductory rate of $200. Please call Kelly at 626-795-3355 for details and information.
