The Pasadena Chamber presents informational webinars for our members. We also make available events and activities of the Chamber and our partners.

Here are Chamber events, from the most recent.

The Pasadena Chamber presented our 8th Economic and Business Summit in October, 2022. Economist Charles Dougherty, Pasadena Economic Development Director David Klug and Patrick Church and Alexandra Sesi of JLL discussed the economy, local development and emerging industries in Pasadena. Mayor Victor Gordo welcomed guests. Chamber Board Chair Raphael Henderson hosted the afternoon's event.

Sponsors of the Economic and Business Summit were Wells Fargo, Verizon and Help With my Loan.

 

 

The Pasadena Chamber and Pasadena Media hosted a candidate forum in advance of the June, 2022, local election. We partnered with 

Community Women Vital Voices

-First AME Church

-the Interdenominational Ministerial Alliance

-The Junior League of Pasadena

-League of Women Voters, Pasadena Area and

-The Pasadena Branch of the NAACP

Many thanks to Board Chair Raphael Henderson of Wells Fargo who moderated expertly. 

Thanks also to Chamber Board Member Charlotte Lacey of Pasadena Humane and the Junior League of Pasadena who organized the event for us.

The City of Pasadena and the Pasadena Chamber of Commerce hosted our fourth Business and Economic Summit at the University Club of Pasadena on October 25, 2016. Tracy Hernandez of the Los Angeles County Business Federation (BizFed) presented information on issues, concerns and the outlook of business in the region. Eric Duyshart of the City of Pasadena highlighted the Pasadena economy and some select development projects in the city. Karl Bird of JPL moderated a panel that included Kim Sidders of Bolton & Company, Brittany Dodd of CBRE and Pauletta Tonilas of LA METRO.
 
You can watch the video here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zM0CU_d5Zjo
 

The City of Pasadena and the Pasadena Chamber of Commerce hosted the third Business and Economic Summit at the University Club in Pasadena on October 29, 2015. Pasadena Chamber Board Chair Tom Daly was Master of Ceremonies. Featured speakers Pasadena Mayor Terry Tornek, San Gabriel Valley Economic Partnership CEO Cynthia Kurtz and Pasadena Economic Development Manager Eric Duyshart presented information on the state of the local and regional economy and gave a brief outlook for the future. A panel of local industry leaders was moderated by Mr. Duyshart. Panelists were Art Center College of Design President Lorne M. Buchman, President and Chief Operating Officer of East West Bank Julia S. Gouw and Parsons Corporation Director of Sustainability Randy Britt. The event was presented by the Pasadena Chamber and the City of Pasadena. Wells Fargo was a sponsor of the Business and Economic Summit 2015. You can view event here:

ACCESS: Opportunity provided a chance for our small bsuienss members to learn first-hand how to become vendors for our large company members. On March 20, 2013, the Pasadena Chamber hosted ACCESS: Opportunity, a procurement forum designed to introduce small businesses to potential large business customers. Presenters from The City of Pasadena, Caltech, Jet Propulsion Laboratory and Parsons were present to explain procurement procedures and how Pasadena Chamber small business members can do business with their companies. In addition, Art Center College of Design, Pasadena City College and the Foothill WIB were on hand to provide information, as well. The Foothill Workforce Investment Board sponsored ACCESS: Opportunity.
